RIESLING

Niagara-on-the-Lake

Originating in the Rhine region, this white is a jack of all trades that highlights its terroir. With notes of tree fruits, including apple and lime. Dating back to the 15th century, this wine is best enjoyed fresh. While it can be aged due to its high acidity, doing so has been known to produce notes of petroleum. This niagara Riesling has a unique richness from the warmth of its summertime production.
